Ogen, Eyesight Maintenance

Wall module that aims to help maintain vision health in our digital world. Reduce eye-strain by following the “vision point” around the form throughout the day. I made a circular physical model to show proof of my concept and used arduino + magnets to make the vision point move. I have also included a rendering of the finished design.
User Sequence:
(a) Person working on computer without a break, suffers from significant eye strain as a result.
(b) Taking an eye-break allows the person to avoid significant eye strain.
