Acrilic and Watercolor

This is an acrylic still-life I made during a painting class At Muhlenberg College.

At Muhlenberg college I was an art editor for Muses, the literary magazine. My work that was published all featured rabbits, which quickly became an inside joke among the staff.

This is the first time that I overlaid my art with digital strokes from an IPad. I added highlights and cleaned up some little mistakes I wasn't happy with.
