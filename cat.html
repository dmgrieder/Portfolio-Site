Lochli

Cat toy with interchangeable pieces to keep cats mentally and physically stimulated. The slim body makes it easy for cats to roll around holding the whole piece, the holes allow them to peek in at the movable components, and the bungee cord ensures that the movable components make noise when knocked against the body of the toy. The holes also allow for interaction between the cat and their carer.
