MFA Thesis

Bunny Burrow is a dollhouse that takes the form of a curtain with interchangeable room panels that easily rolls into a built-in tote bag for use on trips, sleepovers, or simply in another room of the home. This dollhouse is an immersive, tactile, three-dimensional world that allows children to create their own play narratives, while developing their own identities and strengthening their relationships with friends and family members. It comes with a family of little rabbits, plus furnishings and accessories.
