Lights as Communication

Lighting that enhances connectivity in deaf/hearing mixed families through the use of a customizable “code” conveyed through color and pulse frequency. This is a family of objects made up of one table top module, one wall module, and a wearable wrist piece that all work together to send color signals to each other. This project would be wireless, but was made using wires and arduino to show proof of concept.
