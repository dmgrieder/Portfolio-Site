Illustrations for GMWS

These are some examples of promotional material I illustrated for Green Meadow Waldorf School (GMWS). I have been asked to create artwork for class plays, community events, and the school as a whole, since 2013. For GMWS I work primarily with watercolor paint, pencils, and black pen. Their design team adds text as they see fit.My inspiration for these pieces is based on my childhood and the visual media that I enjoyed back in the day.The words “whimsy and warmth” have been used to describe my pieces for GMWS, and this is what I strive for.
